Wellness

There is no greater challenge facing our country than the growing healthcare crisis. If you are a wellness company, or an employer/health plan trying to take better care of your population, you are well aware of the uphill battle you face.

Healthy lifestyle solutions continue to flood the market

42%
of Americans are projected to be obese by the year 2030.

Forty-two percent of Americans are on track to be obese by 2030. Almost 1 out of every 2 American adults are living with at least one chronic illness.

Statistics like these have caused a tidal wave of innovation and attention within the healthcare space. An entire industry has emerged to promote healthy lifestyles and better manage chronic disease. The market is getting crowded.

55% of Americans say they would like to lose weight.

Americans are warming up to wellness

While most Americans aren’t fully honest or aware about their true health status, a growing number are interested in achieving a healthier lifestyle. Recent reports show that 70% of smokers want to quit. And 55% of Americans say they would like to lose weight.

But still, the problem grows worse

Our health statistics as a country aren’t improving, and many wellness initiatives report horribly low participation and mixed results. So, what gives? In many cases, it can be attributed to a breakdown in effective communication. Or a failure to deliver the right solutions to the right individuals at the right time.

We can help you close the gap

Where there is growing supply AND increasing demand, good things can happen. We think we can do our part to close the gap by fueling the success of smart companies and entrepreneurs who deliver solutions with substance.
